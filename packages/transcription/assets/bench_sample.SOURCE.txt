heed hardware-calibration sample
--------------------------------
File:    bench_sample.wav (30s, 16kHz mono) — used only to measure transcription speed.
Source:  "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland", Chapter 1, read by a LibriVox volunteer.
         https://archive.org/details/alice_in_wonderland_librivox
License:  Public Domain (LibriVox dedicates all recordings to the public domain).
         Text by Lewis Carroll (1865), also public domain.
Note:    A ~30s clip of distinct natural speech is required for a representative
         real-time-factor measurement (Whisper processes audio in 30s windows; a tone
         or a looped/short clip mis-measures).
